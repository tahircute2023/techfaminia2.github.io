Words to save me is a game in which you need to complete words to save innocent cats from wild tigers. If your word is correct, then the house and earth will not shake. If your word is wrong, then the house and earth will shake and the tiger will get ready to eat the innocent cat. You have 50 steps to save the cat in starting the game with every shake the house will go down one step. When you have zero steps, then the tiger will eat the innocent cat. To save the innocent cat from a tiger, you need to get hints and steps. So the question is how you will get the hints and steps You can get hints and steps by watching ads and you can also buy them easily. When you have hints and steps, then you can add more hints and steps to your game to save an innocent cat. You have 50 steps by default. You can add 50 steps maximum to one level. Then a total of 100 steps you have at every level to save an innocent cat from a wild tiger..



You have 0 hints by default. You can add 10,000 hints and more hints you can add to the game as you need. The last 4 levels of games are near impossible to finish. If you are intelligent enough, then you can finish those levels. In the last 4 levels, hints and steps will disappear. You need to complete words without any help. The game is challenging at every level but more challenging in the last 4 levels.

Features:-
1- every level has a different background and a different cat.
2- Every level has challenging words.
3- Every level will play the sound when the house and earth will shake.
4- Every level will play the sound when a tiger wants to catch the cat.
5- The last 4 levels are so challenging.
6- in this game ads will not shown every time. (on level start or finish and level click etc it will not be shown).
7- whenever user want to watch ads they can easily watch ads from watch ads button we will not force the our game user.
